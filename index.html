EPS-TOPIK Eligibility and Documentary Requirements

Requirement Category Details
Age 18 to 38 years old on the date of pre-registration.
Health Medically fit to work based on Korean government standards. No history of infectious diseases (e.g., TB, HIV) or physical handicaps that may hamper work efficiency.
Criminal Record No criminal record of imprisonment or a heavier punishment.
Immigration Status No record of deportation or overstay in Korea. Not restricted from leaving the Philippines.
DMW e-Registration Must have an active and updated DMW e-Registration account.
Educational Attainment At least a high school graduate.
Documents Required Valid Passport (at least 1 year validity), PSA-issued Birth Certificate, NBI Clearance, Passport-sized Photo (JPG/PNG), Scanned Passport Copy (JPG/PNG).

Note: Requirements may change. Always check the latest advisories from the Department of Migrant Workers (DMW).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the EPS-TOPIK?

A: The EPS-TOPIK is the official Korean language and skills test for foreign workers who wish to work in Korea under the Employment Permit System.

Q: How much is the exam fee?

A: The exam fee is approximately USD 24. Payment is made directly to HRD Korea's authorized payment channels during the official registration period.

Q: Where can I find the official DMW announcements?

A: All official announcements regarding the EPS-TOPIK exam, including schedules and results, are posted on the DMW website.

Q: Do I need to have a passport to register?

A: Yes, a valid passport is a mandatory requirement for both online and in-person registration. The details on your passport must match the information on your DMW e-Registration profile.

Q: Is this website an official DMW or HRD Korea site?

A: No, this is an informational website. The official and only platform for registration is through the DMW and HRD Korea's websites. This site serves as a helpful guide and information hub.
